# Privacy Policy for Pixel Grid

**Last updated:** December 2025

## Data Collection

Pixel Grid does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data or user information.

## Local Storage

Pixel Grid stores your grid settings (dimensions, colors, opacity, offsets) locally on your device using Chrome's `chrome.storage.local` API. This data:
- Remains on your device only
- Is never sent to external servers
- Can be deleted at any time by uninstalling the extension

## Permissions

Pixel Grid requires the following permissions:
- **activeTab**: To inject the grid overlay on the current webpage
- **storage**: To save your settings locally on your device

## Third-Party Services

Pixel Grid does not use any third-party services, analytics, or tracking tools.

## Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, please contact the developer.
